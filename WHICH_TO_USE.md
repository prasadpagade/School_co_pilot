## My Recommendation for Your Use Case

**Use Render** because:
1. **Simpler setup** - Easier for first-time deployment
2. **Free tier is generous** - 750 hours/month is plenty
3. **Good for sharing** - Family can use it, occasional sleep is fine
4. **Easy to upgrade** - Can switch to paid plan later if needed

**However**, if your ex-wife and step mom will use it frequently and need instant responses, **Railway** might be better.
